[ PITCH CORRECTION · ABLETON LIVE ]

PITCH:IT

No Melodyne, no ARA, no bouncing out -- a native Ableton Live extension that tunes any audio clip right where you work. Right-click a clip and a note editor opens inside Live: drag notes to pitch, shape the vibrato, hear it live, and apply a clean tuned copy to a new track. It re-pitches the actual waveform, so formants and timbre stay intact -- vocals, leads, synths, bass, monophonic or polyphonic. Your original is never touched.

  • Drag to pitch + snap to scale
  • Vibrato flatten / exaggerate
  • Formant-preserving re-pitch
  • 6 detection algorithms
  • Live preview before you commit
  • Non-destructive new track
  • Extract MIDI from audio
  • Reads your key & scale
  • 2 devices per license
  • Live 12.4.5 beta+ (Extensions)
