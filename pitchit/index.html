[ What it does ]

Pitch correction

Drag notes to retune; snaps to the nearest semitone (hold Ctrl/Shift for fine), double-click to snap to your scale, or Snap All at once.

Vibrato control

Flatten, keep, or exaggerate the vibrato of any note - per note, not a blunt global effect.

6 detection algorithms

Vocal, Universal, Leads, Solo, Chords, Fast. Switch instantly to fit the source.

Live preview

Hold a note to audition it, and hear the pitch move as you drag. Decide before you commit.

Sounds natural

Re-pitches the actual waveform, so formants and timbre stay intact. Vocals, leads, synths, bass - mono or poly.

Non-destructive

Apply renders a tuned copy to a new track at the original volume. Untouched notes stay bit-for-bit identical. Extract MIDI too.

Requires Ableton Live 12 with Extensions (12.4.5 beta or higher) OS Windows 10/11 & macOS Note internet connection (license/trial verified online)
